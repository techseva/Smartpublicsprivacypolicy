2. Information Collection and Use

No Personal Data Collection

Smart Public Services App does not collect, store, or process any personal information from users.

Any information entered by the user is submitted directly to official third-party websites accessed through the app.

3. Camera Permission Usage

The app may request access to the device camera only when explicitly initiated by the user.

  • Uploading documents or images

Camera access is never automatic and is always under user control.

  • The camera is never accessed in the background
  • No photos or videos are stored by the app
  • No camera data is collected or shared

Internet access is required for the app to function properly. It is used solely to load and display official public service websites.

6. Children’s Privacy

7. Data Security

Since the app does not collect or store personal data, there is no risk of user data being exposed or misused by the app itself.
