Smart Public Services is designed with privacy in mind. Here's what you need to know about data collection:

Since we do not collect personal data, we have nothing to sell, share, or misuse. Your privacy is fully protected when using Smart Public Services.

3. Data Security

While Smart Public Services does not collect personal data, we take security seriously:

  • Secure Connections: All links to government websites use secure HTTPS connections whenever available to protect your data during transmission.

Please note that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to provide a secure experience, we cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted through third-party websites.
